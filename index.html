== Installation == From within WordPress 1. Visit Plugins > Hit (Add New) button. 2. Search for 'GD Player'. 3. Hit the (Install now) button. 4. After installing, hit the (Active) button. Manually 1. Visit Plugins > Hit (Add New) button. 2. Hit (Upload Plugin) Button. 3. Hit (Choose File) Button 4. Select (gd-player.zip) and Hit the (Install now) button. 5. After installing, hit the (Active) button.
